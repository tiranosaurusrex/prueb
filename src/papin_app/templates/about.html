Javier Coll

Hospitality executive and transformative dealmaker with over 30 years of experience in global resort and leisure sectors. As co-founder of Apple Leisure Group (ALG) and its flagship brand AMResorts, he reshaped the all-inclusive resort model.

Under Javier's leadership, ALG expanded significantly across Latin America, the Caribbean, and Europe, growing from a few properties to over 100 resorts. He orchestrated major investment and M&A transactions, including strategic partnerships with Bain Capital and KKR.

The acquisition of ALG by Hyatt Hotels was a landmark event in the global hospitality industry, with Javier playing a central role as one of its key architects.

Domingo Rex

Senior financial advisor and strategic practitioner with over 25 years of experience across banking, credit, and investment advisory in Europe and Latin America.

Previously held senior roles at Banco CAM, including Managing Director and Country Head in Mexico, where he managed a tourism-focused credit portfolio of nearly $2 billion. Also advised major Spanish banks during strategic divestments.

His advisory experience includes supporting private investors and institutional clients in tourism and real estate transactions across Mexico and the Caribbean, known for technical expertise and consistent delivery.
