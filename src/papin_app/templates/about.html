Javier Coll

Co-Founder & Strategic Advisor

Javier is a hospitality executive and transformative dealmaker with over 30 years of experience in global resort and leisure sectors. As co-founder of Apple Leisure Group (ALG) and its flagship brand AMResorts, he reshaped the all-inclusive resort model, transforming it into a premium, investment-grade hospitality platform.

Under Javier's leadership, ALG expanded significantly across Latin America, the Caribbean, and Europe, growing from a few properties to over 100 resorts. He orchestrated major investment and M&A transactions, including strategic partnerships with Bain Capital and KKR, and multiple acquisitions across the hospitality value chain, positioning ALG as a vertically integrated market leader.

The acquisition of ALG by Hyatt Hotels was a landmark event in the global hospitality industry, with Javier playing a central role as one of its key architects. He remained with Hyatt to oversee integration and expansion, driving substantial international growth.

Domingo Rex

Co-Founder & Financial Advisor

Domingo is a senior financial advisor and strategic practitioner with over 25 years of experience across banking, credit, and investment advisory in Europe and Latin America. He has advised financial institutions, funds, and corporate clients on complex transactions, particularly within the hospitality, real estate, and special situations sectors.

Previously, Domingo held senior roles at Banco CAM, including Managing Director and Country Head in Mexico, where he managed a tourism-focused credit portfolio of nearly $2 billion. He also advised major Spanish banks such as CaixaBank and Bankia during their strategic divestments in Mexico, accelerating execution while preserving shareholder value.

His advisory experience includes supporting private investors such as Rodina, guiding and executing its initial hospitality investments, as well as assisting banks and institutional clients in tourism and real estate transactions across Mexico and the Caribbean.
