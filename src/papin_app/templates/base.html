Deep Expertise, Strategic Insight

Javier Coll

Hospitality executive and transformative dealmaker with over 30 years of experience in global resort and leisure sectors. As co-founder of Apple Leisure Group (ALG) and its flagship brand AMResorts, he reshaped the all-inclusive resort model, transforming it into a premium, investment-grade hospitality platform.

Under Javier's leadership, ALG expanded significantly across Latin America, the Caribbean, and Europe, growing from a few properties to over 100 resorts. He orchestrated major investment and M&A transactions, including strategic partnerships with Bain Capital and KKR, and multiple acquisitions across the hospitality value chain, positioning ALG as a vertically integrated market leader.

The acquisition of ALG by Hyatt Hotels was a landmark event in the global hospitality industry, with Javier playing a central role as one of its key architects. He remained with Hyatt to oversee integration and expansion, driving substantial international growth.

Throughout his career, Javier has demonstrated broad-based leadership across operations, commercial strategy, branding, and global development, consistently serving as a strategic visionary shaping the direction of entire organizations.

Now, as co-founder of CRSA, he leverages his extensive general management expertise, visionary outlook, and deep transactional experience to guide investors, owners, and operators through complex strategic challenges.

Domingo Rex

Senior financial advisor and strategic practitioner with over 25 years of experience across banking, credit, and investment advisory in Europe and Latin America. He has advised financial institutions, funds, and corporate clients on complex transactions, particularly within the hospitality, real estate, and special situations sectors.

Previously, Domingo held senior roles at Banco CAM, including Managing Director and Country Head in Mexico, where he managed a tourism-focused credit portfolio of nearly $2 billion. He also advised major Spanish banks such as CaixaBank and Bankia during their strategic divestments in Mexico, accelerating execution while preserving shareholder value.

His advisory experience includes supporting private investors such as Rodina, guiding and executing its initial hospitality investments, as well as assisting banks and institutional clients in tourism and real estate transactions across Mexico and the Caribbean.

Domingo has served on the boards of companies within these sectors, offering strategic direction through growth, restructuring, and capital deployment. He is known for his technical expertise, regional insight, honesty, clarity, and consistent delivery.

Now, as co-founder of CRSA, Domingo partners with investors, institutions, and operators to assess opportunities, structure complex deals, and create lasting value across evolving markets.
